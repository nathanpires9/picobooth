More Information

What is PicoBooth?

PicoBooth is a fun and customizable online photo booth that allows you to capture moments with various fun stickers, backgrounds, and filters! It’s perfect for any event, whether it’s a wedding, party, or just for fun!

How to Use PicoBooth

Simply visit our site, take a photo, customize it by adding stickers and effects, and then save or share your personalized photo strip. It's easy, fun, and completely free!

Features

  • Instant photo capture with a built-in camera feature.
  • Choose from a variety of stickers and effects.
  • Download or share your photos instantly.
  • Customizable photo strip layout.
  • Easy-to-use interface for all ages.

Support

If you need help or have any questions, feel free to visit our Support Page. Our team is ready to assist you.
